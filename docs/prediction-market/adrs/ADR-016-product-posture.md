# ADR-016 — Experimental, value-bearing, permissionless/no-entity posture

**Status:** Accepted 2026-07-16; issue #26 legal/operational-readiness evidence gate remains open
**Decision:** Globally permissionless contracts, no operating entity, experimental intent, and explicit real-JUNO/participant-specific risk disclosures.

## Alternatives

- describe JUNO as valueless play credits;
- infer no legal responsibility from no entity;
- accurately separate protocol access from interface/participant duties.

## Evidence

JUNO is transferable collateral and governance power. Legal treatment is current, jurisdictional, and actor-specific; R5 cites a 2026 U.S. proposed rule only as evidence of change.

## Consequences

Contributors, hosts, creators, users, keepers, and governance participants seek their own advice. Reference interface uses a published content/discovery policy. Contracts do not pretend to enforce identity/geography.

## Safe default and revisit

No public interface/deployment until dated counsel advice. Refresh on actor, law, geography, asset, fee, or hosting change.
